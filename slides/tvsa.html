"Embedding tech skills in the organisation"

Identify a “product manager” within each organisation and train them.

A product manager?

"The CEO of the product", the most important "technical" skill needed by any organisation developing digital services.

"Sharing learning"

Organise product managers into groups of 2-3; Assign each group a "product team".

The product team

2 developers

supported by a team lead

Assigned to work with 2-3 groups, switching between them every 1-2 weeks, during the Discovery phase
