What about <dialog>?

Popovers

  • Dismissed by clicking ouside or "esc" therefore less intrusive
  • Great for Toasts, Action Menu*, Tooltips
  • Javascript Not Required

Dialogs

  • Must be explicitly closed
  • Great for Alerts, Confirmation, required interactions
  • Needs Javascript (open and close)
